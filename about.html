About Us

The Industry Discourse, TID, is the largest student-led energy conference in Sub-Saharan Africa, which aims to create collaboration and inspire students, academia, and energy professionals to work together to develop long-term solutions to Africa’s energy problems.

TID was conceived in 2019 by students of the Department of Petroleum and Gas Engineering at the University of Lagos with one goal: to bridge the gap between students and the energy industry while crafting an ecosystem where energy interests solidify into measurable impact. Over the years, we have successfully organized 3 editions and intend to do more to continuously foster collaboration between the organizations in the energy industry and academia to catalyze much-needed change.

Mission

Motivating generations of forward-thinkers to drive disruptive innovative solutions in the energy landscape

Vision

To foster a synergistic platform where the next energy solution stems from

Our Impact

Since TID’s inception in 2019, we have had three successful editions. The last edition, 3.0, witnessed young minds providing innovative solutions to tackle the multifaceted dimensions of Africa’s energy crisis across different sectors with participation from universities in 9 different countries across Africa: Nigeria, Ghana, Benin, Ethiopia, Madagascar, Malawi, Rwanda, Kenya and Namibia.

Our Team
