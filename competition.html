Competition

Ignite your passion and unleash your potential with InnovateX, this edition's Energy Startup pitch competition designed to develop groundbreaking solutions to Africaâ€™s energy challenges. The theme for TID 4.0, Energy Nexus, is significant to InnovateX as it focuses on the intersection of energy innovation and sustainability, fostering collaboration between young minds with fresh ideas and seasoned professionals who possess the resources to transform promising concepts into reality.

InnovateX challenges teams of 3-5 undergraduates to develop innovative solutions to address energy issues in Africa. Whether your solution revolves around digital platforms or tangible hardware, we encourage creativity and ingenuity in tackling energy access, transportation, clean cooking, industrial efficiency, and more!

Download the handbook to get a full overview of the competition journey, from team formation to the final pitch event. We encourage you to carefully review these details and use the information provided to build a winning solution.
